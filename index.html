FAQ

How can I find out your rates?

To calculate the shipping cost, please provide a detailed description of the shipment. You can do this in any convenient way: by e-mail, by phone, via viber, or via the feedback form.

Are your rates final and certain?

Your shipping quote is based on the information you provide to us at the time of booking. Due to the high volume we ship through our network of carriers, we are able to secure deeply discounted shipping rates.

Is the cargo insured?

The cargo is insured at the request of the client. Insurance covers loss or shortage of cargo.
